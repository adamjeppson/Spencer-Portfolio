This song is Self produced, with most of the tracking done at the UVU DAPR studios. I started with Drums, and caught 14 channels of audio. Not all channels made the final cut but having options was nice. I used mostly dynamic mics on close placements and condenser stereo pairs for room and overheads. Super happy with the drum tone achieved here, real punchy and the perfect centerpiece for this mix. Guitars were layered with a second take that’s a little hidden in the mix but providing a lot of low end and filling in the upper registers of the bass and lower registers of the other guitar track. Bass was simul-recorded with the main take of guitars and DI’ed in the studio then duplicated in mixing, and a cab sim applied to the duplicate. Bass tone sounds good in the exposed parts of this track, as well in the mix. Vocals were done with a close and far pair of condenser mics.
