I self produced this track, and I’m really pleased with how it came out. Guitars Drums and Bass were done at the UVU DAPR studios with a rather extensive setup for guitars. The guitarist brought in two half stacks and I miced each differently and used each amp tone to cover different ground. Stereo condenser rooms helped provide a little context in the final mix. A second layer of guitar is present to help hold things down while the main guitar takes noodly leads and upper register chords. Bass was split across two tracks, an amp DI and a cab sim, the cab sim compressed and low passed pretty aggressively to ensure the low end doesn’t go anywhere, while the DI gave punch and upper register sparkle. BG vocals have a little more room mic in the mix than the lead to separate them a little.
